About

"Leave no one behind"

That is the motto of the Sustainable Development Goals- summarily meaning that everyone should be given equal rights and equal oppurtunities. We aim to tackle this goal of inclusivity with a simple solution, Jagora. "Jagora" is a hausa word meaning "leadership." It is a volunteering platform that allows people to take charge and make a difference in their own communities, leaving no one behind even in the changemaking process. Currently focused on the northern part of Nigeria, or Arewa, Jagora places emphasis on 4 SDG goals that hold a special importance to Arewa communities. It will allow citizens to register under a supervisor in a center that is closes to them. Once registered, they can sign up for outreach and volunteer work. They may head their own volunteer work or work under umbrella organisations such as Women At Risk International Foundation (WARIF), Lift Above Poverty Foundation (LAPO), etc. This enables everyone to dedicate their time and resources into changing our society for the better- together.
